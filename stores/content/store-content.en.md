# Geopoto

## Title

Geopoto

## Subtitle

Geography that sticks

## Description

Geopoto helps you learn the world without needing a connection. Practice flags, capitals, country names and map positions through quick sessions, daily challenges and a calm atlas made for real memorization.

Train your way: choose the region you want to focus on, mix question and answer formats, set a question limit or keep going in infinite mode. Geopoto can ask from a country name, a capital, a flag or a map position, then let you answer with the format you want to practice.

Adaptive Difficulty keeps practice useful by prioritizing the countries and formats you miss most often, while still keeping sessions varied. Your practice history stays on your device and can be reset at any time.

The Daily Challenge gives you one fresh geography puzzle per day, with streak tracking and optional local reminders. It is perfect for building a small habit without turning learning into homework.

The Learn area is your offline geography base: explore 197 countries, browse flags, search capitals, inspect country details and use the interactive map to connect names with places. Flag filters by color make it easier to compare patterns and spot details you might otherwise miss.

Geopoto is available in English, French, German, Spanish, Italian and Portuguese, and you can choose a separate language for geographic names if you want to study the world from another angle.
